A Note on Wallet Standards Discovery

Nobody wants to agree to a wallet API standard before it's living.

  • General apathy/disinterest.
  • Every team is already super busy.
  • Good ideas will gain traction anyways.
  • The trick is to avoid overlapping namespaces, and ensure iterations of proposals are locked to specific method names.
  • Maybe we should lock method names to EIP hashes.

EIP 712: signTypedData_v3

We have shipped the latest spec of the signTypedData proposal, allowing faster iteration on contracts that require efficiently validated signatures that are also human readable.
